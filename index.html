What does the future look like?

Predicting semantic segmentation frames of traffic environments.
The data is obtained using the CARLA simulator at 5 fps.

Samples

Conditioned on 10 initial frames. Image dimensions are 3x64x64




Evaluation

Mean IoU from a selection of classes. The measurements are from 192 sequences with a confidence
interval of 95%. Some critical classes, like vehicles and pedestrians, are barely represented.
This might be due to the fact that they are small by size and seldom occur in the training set.
The mean IoU is still strong since there are a few dominating classes which are predicted very well.

The thought behind this plot/metric is to investigate how far into the future it is convenient (useful) to predict what will happen. As expected, the mean IoU decreases over time.
